IHY

back in 2020 Dell Colombia was struggling with customers information their employees were writing in their database, delivers where taken to wrong addresses or even shipped to places that did not exist in Colombia. This issue cost money, time, and customers' loyalty to the company. That is why my brother who is Outbound Fulfillments and logistics senior at Dell asked me to find a solution using technology that could fit the organization. With this in mind, I started research on address validation, as it is hard to use Colombia´s address database and it is not completely open source we started to explore Google cloud services. With this project I practiced Html, CSS, JavaScript, and modern technologies implementation with google Apis, eventually we deployed the platform that scaled to Mexico and Perú.
